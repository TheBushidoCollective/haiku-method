# H·AI·K·U Brand Constants

## Name

- **Full name:** H·AI·K·U
- **Expanded:** Human AI Knowledge Unification
- **Stylized:** H·AI·K·U (with middle dots separating the acronym components)
- **Plain text:** HAIKU (ALL CAPS when dots aren't available)
- **Never:** "Haiku" when referring to the framework

## Tagline Options

1. **"Disciplined form. Unified intelligence."**
2. **"Structure that scales from intent to insight."**
3. **"Constrained form. Clear outcomes."**

## Domain

- **Primary:** haikumethod.ai

## Relationships

- **AI-DLC** is the software development profile of H·AI·K·U. It implements H·AI·K·U's 4-phase lifecycle with git workflows, test suites, pull requests, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment gates.
- **SWARM** (Scope, Workstreams, Accountability, Results, Memory) validates H·AI·K·U's universality by applying the framework to marketing and sales domains.
- **H·AI·K·U** is the universal core — AI-DLC and SWARM are domain-specific profiles that inherit its lifecycle, principles, and collaboration model.

## Visual Identity Direction

- Minimal and clean
- Japanese-inspired aesthetic sensibility
- Clean typography with generous whitespace
- Restrained color palette — monochrome with selective accent (teal)
- Structural motifs reflecting constraint and clarity (grids, balanced composition)
- No decorative excess; every element serves a purpose
- Middle dot (·) as the connecting element in the logo — separates the human (H), the AI, and the knowledge unification (K·U)
